## Security Architecture

### Local-First Security
Our architecture ensures maximum security through complete local processing:

- **On-Device Processing** — All transcription happens locally
- **Offline Capability** — Full functionality without internet
- **Zero External Transfer** — No data leaves your device
- **Local AI** — All AI analysis runs on your machine
