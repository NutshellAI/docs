## Installation & Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="download" title="How do I install Nutshell?">
    Nutshell can be downloaded from our official website. After downloading, simply run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions. The app will guide you through the initial setup process.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="gear" title="What are the system requirements?">
    Nutshell is designed to run efficiently on modern computers. Since all processing happens locally, we recommend:
    - A multi-core processor
    - Minimum 8GB RAM
    - Windows 10/11 or macOS 10.15+
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="trash" title="How do I uninstall Nutshell?">
    You can uninstall Nutshell like any other application:
    - On Windows: Use "Add or Remove Programs"
    - On macOS: Drag the application to the Trash
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features & Usage

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="microphone" title="How does real-time transcription work?">
    Nutshell captures audio from your system and transcribes it in real-time using on-device AI technology. You can start transcribing by clicking the record button or using keyboard shortcuts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="robot" title="What can I ask the AI chatbot?">
    The AI chatbot can help you with:
    - Summarizing meeting content
    - Extracting action items
    - Finding specific information from past discussions
    - Generating meeting minutes
    Simply type your question or use "/" to access preset prompts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="wifi-slash" title="Does Nutshell work offline?">
    Yes! All processing happens locally on your device, so Nutshell works perfectly without an internet connection. You can transcribe meetings and interact with the AI chatbot completely offline.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="shield" title="How secure is my data with Nutshell?">
    Nutshell processes everything locally on your device. No audio, transcriptions, or chat data ever leaves your computer. This ensures maximum privacy and security for your sensitive information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="building" title="Is Nutshell suitable for enterprise use?">
    Yes! Nutshell is ideal for organizations with strict security requirements since all data stays on your device. There's no cloud storage or external server communication involved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="scale-balanced" title="What are the legal considerations for recording meetings?">
    Users are responsible for complying with local laws regarding recording conversations. Always inform all participants when recording or transcribing meetings and obtain necessary consent. Nutshell provides reminder notifications when starting transcription.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="microphone-slash" title="What if transcription is not working?">
    First, check that:
    - Your microphone permissions are enabled
    - The correct audio input is selected
    - You have sufficient system resources available
    If issues persist, try restarting the application.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="memory" title="How can I improve transcription accuracy?">
    To get the best results:
    - Use a good quality microphone
    - Ensure clear audio input
    - Minimize background noise
    - Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  </Accordion>
